Dr. Josué Daniel Rivera Fernández is a Mechatronics engineer graduated from the Interdisciplinary Professional Unit in Engineering and Advanced Technologies (UPIITA-IPN), Master of Science in Electronic Engineering at the Higher School of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering Zacatenco Unit (ESIME Zac) with specialty in instrumentation from which he graduated with honors and Doctor in Communications and Electronics with a specialty in Photonic Instrumentation in the ESIME Unidad Culhuacan, also graduated with honors. Has participated in the publication of JCR articles, articles indexed to SCOPUS, it also has the title of a patent and is awaiting another whose registration is in the IMPI both in the area of ​​mechatronics - optical instrumentation. His areas of interest are instrumentation, mechatronic and optical systems, artificial intelligence systems applied to biomedical optics, systems energy and smart networks.

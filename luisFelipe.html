Bionic Engineer

Orcid Scopus Personal Web Page

Bionics Engineer graduated from Unidad Profesional Interdisciplinaria en Ingeniería y Tecnologías Avanzadas (UPIITA) of the Instituto Politécnico Nacional through the project "Sleep detection system in motorists through the detection of the change in the parameters of their biological signals." He obtained the degree of Master of Science in Electronic Engineering at the Escuela Superior de Ingeniería Mecánica y Eléctrica (ESIME) Unidad Zacatenco, IPN, carrying out the project "Development of an irradiation system for Photodynamic Therapy with controlled optical dosimetry." In 2015 he obtained the degree of Doctor in Communications and Electronics with a specialty in biophotonics at the ESIME, Unidad Culhuacan, IPN, with the thesis titled "Experimental system for the evaluation of dosimetry in photodynamic therapy." In 2018 he carried out a research stay at the Instituto Tecnológico de Monterrey, campus Monterrey in Competitive and Technological Intelligence. Since 2019, he has been a member of the teaching staff of the Unidad Profesional Interdisciplinaria en Ingeniería campus Hidalgo.

Dr. Diego Adrián Fabila obtained the title of Bionics Engineer at the Unit Interdisciplinary Professional in Engineering and Advanced Technologies (UPIITA) in the in 2008, later in 2010 he obtained the degree of Master of Science in Electronic Engineering at the Higher School of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering Zacatenco Unit (ESIME Zac). In 2014 he obtained the degree of Doctor in Communications and Electronics with a specialty in Photonic Instrumentation in the Higher School of Mechanical and Electrical Engineering Culhuacán Unit (ESIME Which). He is the author of several JCR articles and patents. His research area is focused on the development of optomechatronic systems for the diagnosis and treatment of diseases, r. It is currently part of the National System of Level 1 Researchers and full-time professor in the Professional Unit Interdisciplinary Engineering Campus Hidalgo of the National Polytechnic Institute (UPIIH-IPN).
